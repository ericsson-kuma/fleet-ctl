Phase 1 — good config v2 reaches the whole fleet

Each wave assigns v2 to its slice, soaks for a 2s health window, and is promoted only after staying inside the failure budget. Hover the marks.

Phase 2 — bad config v3 halted at the 25% wave

The 5% canary happens to pass. In the 25% wave, dev-03 rejects v3: 1 failure / 4 devices = 25% > 20% budget — the guardrail halts the rollout and reverts the 5 touched devices to v2. Wave 2 never starts.
